Lab 5 – HTML Image Map

Demonstrates clickable areas: circle, rect, poly on a single image using <map> and <area> tags

① Circle Area – shape="circle"

Syntax: <area shape="circle" coords="cx,cy,radius">

② Rectangle Area – shape="rect"

Syntax: <area shape="rect" coords="x1,y1,x2,y2">

③ Polygon Area – shape="poly"

Syntax: <area shape="poly" coords="x1,y1,x2,y2,x3,y3,...">

④ Mixed Map – circle, rect, and poly on one image

One <map> with multiple <area> tags of different shapes.
